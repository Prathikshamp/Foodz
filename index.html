Veg Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 Chicken drumsticks/breast
  • 100 ml Curd
  • 1 Egg (beaten)
  • 50 gms All purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp Chilli powder
  • 1 tsp White pepper powder
  • 1 tsp Onion (dried) powdered
  • 1 tsp Basil/tulsi leaves (dried)
  • 1 tsp Oregano / omam leaves (dried), powdered
  • 1 Green chilli, chopped
  • 1 tsp Garlic (dried)
  • 1 tsp Ginger (dried)
  • taste Salt
  • Oil (for frying)

Ingredients

Fiery and flavour packed, this is one of our classics and always a hit. Chicken legs marinated in a flavorsome paste and then grilled on the tandoor to perfection. The juicy and full of spice chicken pieces just fill you up with its delectable taste and makes this dish of Tandoori Chicken a must have snack at a dinner party with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams chicken liver
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 4 green chilli
  • 1 large tomato
  • 2 stalks coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup

Ingredients

2 lbs. sliced frigate tuna or of another firm-fleshed white fish Cornstarch, to dredge fish Vegetable oil, for cooking
